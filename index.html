Qualitative Research

My primary research project is in meta-research on qualitative research in software engineering. I am investigating how software engineering researchers conduct qualitative research, how they perceive qualitative methods, and how to increase awareness and acceptance of qualitative research in software engineering.

Data Visualization

I am a member of the Lodestone project in the CHISEL lab, a customizable tool for big data visualization. My role is primarily as an interaction and visualization designer for use cases. I am currently working on custom visualizations for our current use case for the tool, which uses big data visualizations to investigate Android malware.

General Interests

My general research interests revolve around human-computer interaction. I am particularly interested in finding new ways to leverage technology to support healthy living. Some future research ideas are to determine if social commentary can be used to assist people in understanding articles about health and fitness, and developing simplified interfaces to help people adapt to new technologies at an older age.
